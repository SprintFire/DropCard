Idea Starter's Hackathon

Meet people, engage and collaborate on ideas to build a MVP.

Join us in our fun filled weekend event to build your startup in just 48 hours. Form or join a team, validate your idea, market research and build a minimal viable product. Demo your prototypes and receive valuable feedback from a panel of experts. Winners announced and new friends made!

Our event is centered around action, innovation and education. Get feedback from industry experts, find a co-founder or find team members with specific skill sets.

With the help of Idea Starter and the institutions involved, the winning teams will be given the opportunity to bring their concept forward.

Our first event is focused on the financial services sector. Learn more about FinTech →

Event Schedule

  • Friday
  • Saturday
  • Sunday

Friday


6:30pm Registration Arrive at our venue and get checked in.
6:45pm Dinner & Networking Eat food and get to know fellow participants.
7:15pm Welcome & Speakers Review agenda for the weekend and introduction to speakers, advisors and community leaders. Followed by talks by industry experts addressing issues and problems they face in the industry and where innovation is necessary.
8:00pm 60 Second Idea Pitches Individuals with ideas pitch their ideas under a minute to gather interest from others to join their team.
8:45pm Voting & Teams Formation Top ideas are voted and teams of maximum 6 members with various skills are formed around the best ideas.
9:15pm Start Working Discussion of skills in the team and roles of everyone in the team are discussed and planned out for the weekend.
10:00pm Wrap up Venue closed for the day. Teams can continue to work outside of the venue if they wish.

Saturdayth


9:00am Breakfast Arrive, simple breakfast and coffee.
9:30am Start Working Start working on the idea as a team to develop a MVP over the weekend and validate your idea.
11:00am Workshops Workshops that some team members can attend if they consider it to be useful for their idea.
12:00pm Lunch
1:00pm Check-in Status reports and call for help.
2:00pm Advisor meetings Industry experts help teams one-on-one to help develop the idea, provide feedback and direct the idea in the right direction.
6:30pm Dinner
7:30pm Check-in Status reports and call for help.
10:00pm Wrap-up Finished for the day.

Sundayth


9:00am Breakfast Arrive, simple breakfast and coffee.
10:00am Check-in Status report and call for help.
12:00pm Lunch
1:00pm Pitch Coaching Talk on what should be included and how to structure your final pitch.
3:00pm Practice Pitch & Tech Check Practice pitch session to get final feedback on pitches and checking tech used for presentation.
4:00pm Dinner
5:30pm Final Presentations Each team presents their idea and MVP with a 5 minute pitch followed by a 3 minutes of Q&A by the judges. Presentations are done before a panel of judges, all the teams and visitors.
7:30pm Judging & Awards Winners announced and awards are distributed. Followed by vote of thanks.
8:30pm Networking & Wrap-up
9:00pm Home Time!
